Dabong, Kuala Krai, Kelantan
DABONG

Dabong, a hidden gem in Kelantan, Malaysia, beckons with not only its natural wonders but also unique attractions like Gua Ikan, Stong Hill Resort, and Jelawang Pipe Resort. Venture into the depths of Gua Ikan, a captivating cave with intricate formations, offering a spelunking adventure that reveals the mysteries of the underground world. Stong Hill Resort provides a cozy retreat amidst the lush surroundings, offering breathtaking views of the Kelantan River and the Taman Negara rainforest.

For a distinctive experience, Jelawang Pipe Resort combines luxury with nature, allowing you to unwind in stylish accommodation while being surrounded by the soothing sounds of flowing water from its ingenious water pipe setup. Join us on this vlog as we delve into the unique attractions that make Dabong a diverse and unforgettable destination!

OUR TRIP
Places
Cost
4:25 a.m.

The day kicked off with the soft hum of the train wheels as we departed Wakaf Bharu, embarking on a sunrise-filled journey to Dabong. The anticipation in the air was palpable, setting the stage for a day of adventure and exploration.


7:30 a.m.

The train gracefully pulled into Dabong's station, where we were warmly greeted by our enthusiastic tour guide. The air was crisp, and the surroundings exuded a serene charm that hinted at the wonders awaiting us.


8:00 a.m.

Our first stop, Gua Ikan, unfolded like a page from a history book. The guide painted vivid tales of the cave's past, intertwining legends and facts. Amidst ancient rock formations and mysterious shadows, we immersed ourselves in the captivating atmosphere, capturing the essence of the moment with countless photographs.


8:45 a.m.

Energized by history, we strolled to a nearby restaurant for a delightful breakfast. The aroma of local delicacies wafted through the air, and the warmth of the morning sun added a golden touch to our communal feast.


9:15 a.m.

The next chapter of our adventure awaited at Jelawang Pipe Resort. The Flying Fox experience was nothing short of exhilarating, our laughter echoing through the lush surroundings as we soared above the treetops. The ATV ride that followed was an adrenaline-fueled journey, navigating bumpy and challenging terrains that revealed breathtaking panoramic views of the landscape.

11:50 a.m.

Our convoy then wound its way to Stong Hill Resort, a gateway to a hidden gem – a waterfall nestled in the heart of nature. The hike, though demanding, unfolded like a story, with each step revealing a new chapter in the form of scenic vistas and untouched wilderness.


12:40 p.m.

The payoff was awe-inspiring – a majestic waterfall framed by lush greenery. We couldn't help but be captivated by its beauty. Exhausted but invigorated, we plunged into the refreshing waters, the cool mist rejuvenating our spirits. Our tour guide, a true maestro, treated us to a cup of his self-made coffee, a delightful blend that complemented the natural surroundings.


3:00 p.m.

Reluctantly, we bid farewell to the enchanting waterfall, retracing our steps back to the resort's entrance. Every corner seemed to whisper tales of the day's escapades.


4:00 p.m.

As the day wound down, we boarded the train back to Dabong's station, bringing our day trip to a close. The memories we etched into the canvas of that day would forever be a tapestry of laughter, awe, and the unspoken camaraderie forged amidst the beauty of Kelantan's hidden treasures.
